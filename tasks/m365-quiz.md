## **Scoring Logic & Category Assignment**

### **How Scoring Works**

Each question awards points based on current utilization level. The system calculates scores across six functional areas:

1. **Communication Score** (Q1-Q3): Max 75 points
2. **Collaboration Score** (Q4-Q6): Max 75 points
3. **Productivity Score** (Q7-Q9): Max 75 points
4. **Security Score** (Q10-Q12): Max 75 points
5. **Analytics Score** (Q13-Q14): Max 50 points
6. **Overall M365 Utilization**: Average of all areas
